# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 11, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to FlowTask. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection in compliance with Google Play Store policies and global privacy standards.

## 2. Data We Collect
- **Account Information:** Email address, User ID, Profile name.
- **Task Data:** Task titles, descriptions, due dates, statuses, and completion timestamps.
- **Productivity Analytics:** In-app behavior, focus session durations, app usage statistics, streak tracking.
- **Device Information:** OS version, Device model, App version, unique device identifiers.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
- To provide, maintain, and improve the FlowTask app's core functionality (Task Tracking & Reminders).
- To generate personalized productivity insights and analytics.
- To send transactional notifications and intelligent reminders.
- To detect and prevent security breaches.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use **Firebase Infrastructure** (Google LLC) as our backend provider:
- **Firebase Authentication:** Handles secure user login.
- **Cloud Firestore:** Secure cloud database storing user and task records.
- **Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics:** Collects anonymized usage and crash reports to improve application stability.
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging:** Handles push notifications.

All data handled via Firebase is subject to Google's rigorous compliance and privacy standards.

## 5. User Rights
As a user, you hold the right to:
- Access your data directly from within the app.
- Modify or update incomplete/inaccurate data.
- Request the deletion of your account and all associated task/analytics data via the in-app "Delete Account" setting or by contacting support.
- Opt-out of analytics via app settings (where applicable).

## 6. Developer Responsibilities
FlowTask developers are responsible for:
- Ensuring data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS).
- Limiting backend data access to strict security rules on Firestore.
- Anonymizing analytics data where possible.
- Not selling personal user data to third-party ad networks.

## 7. Contact Us
For privacy-related inquiries, contact compliance@flowtaskapp.com.
