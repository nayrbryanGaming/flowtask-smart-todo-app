# Privacy Policy for FlowTask

**Effective Date: April 13, 2026**

FlowTask ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
*   **Account Credentials:** When you register, we collect your email address and authentication details managed securely via **Firebase Authentication**.
*   **User Content:** We store your tasks, descriptions, and productivity goals in **Google Cloud Firestore**.
*   **Delete Account Data:** Users have the right to request full deletion of their personal data. See Section 5 for details.

### 1.2 Automated Data Collection
*   **Firebase Analytics:** We collect anonymized usage data (e.g., app open frequency, feature engagement) to improve our productivity insights.
*   **Crashlytics:** We collect technical crash reports to ensure app stability.
*   **Device Identifiers:** We may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) for notification delivery via **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)**.

## 2. Purpose of Data Processing
We process your data to:
*   Maintain your task synchronization across devices.
*   Generate personalized productivity analytics.
*   Send intelligent reminders and focus-mode alerts.
*   Comply with legal obligations and security protocols.

## 3. Data Storage & Security
We utilize the Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) for all data storage. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. While we implement industry-standard security measures, no digital storage is 100% secure.

## 4. Third-Party Service Providers
To deliver our high-quality service, we share data with:
*   **Google Firebase:** For hosting, database, and authentication.
*   **Google Analytics:** For behavioral analysis (Anonymized).

## 5. Your Rights & Data Deletion
Under Google Play Store policies and global privacy laws (GDPR/CCPA/LGPD):
*   **Access/Update:** You can view and edit your profile in the app settings.
*   **Data Deletion (MANDATORY):** You may delete your account and all associated data directly via **Settings > Account > Delete Account**. All data in Firestore and Auth will be permanently purged.
*   **Web-Based Deletion:** If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion via our website at [https://flowtask.app/delete-account](https://flowtask.app/delete-account).

## 6. Children's Privacy
FlowTask does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe such data has been collected, contact us immediately.
