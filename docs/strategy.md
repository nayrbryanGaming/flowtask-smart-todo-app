# Product & Growth Strategy: FlowTask

## 1. Value Proposition
"From Chaos to Flow." We don't just list tasks; we help users finish them by understanding their personal productivity cycles.

## 2. Monetization Model: Freemium
- **Tier 1 (Free):** Unlimited tasks, standard Pomodoro timer (25/5), 7-day history.
- **Tier 2 (Premium - $4.99/mo):** Advanced heatmaps, Intelligent Scheduling (AI suggestions), detailed PDF productivity reports, custom notification sounds, and multi-device cloud sync priority.

## 3. Road to 10k Users
### Phase 1: The Foundation (0 - 1k)
- **Product Hunt Launch:** Goal: Top 5 of the day.
- **Direct Outreach:** Target productivity subreddits (r/getdisciplined, r/productivity) and Discord communities.
- **Incentivized Beta:** Free 3-month Premium for early testers who provide detailed feedback.

### Phase 2: Content Engine (1k - 5k)
- **Short-form Video:** TikTok/Instagram Reels showing "Life with vs. without FlowTask."
- **SEO Blog:** "How to find your productive hours using analytics."
- **Partnerships:** Micro-influencers in the "Studygram" and "Freelance" niches.

### Phase 3: The Referral Loop (5k - 10k)
- **"Invite a Friend":** Both get 1 month of Premium free.
- **Shared Challenges:** Users can share "Focus Streaks" on social media with deep-links to join.

## 4. Launch Strategy Checklist
- [ ] Finalize App Store assets (High-res screenshots).
- [ ] Record a 30-second high-impact demo video.
- [ ] Prepare the "Launch Day" press kit.
- [ ] Warm up primary social media handles.

## 5. KPIs & Analytics
- **Primary:** DAU/MAU ratio (Engagement), Retention (Day 30), Conversion Rate (Free to Premium).
- **Secondary:** Avg. Focus Session duration, Task Completion rate per user.
